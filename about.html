Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our process, pricing, and timelines.

How long does a typical branding project take?

Timelines vary depending on scope, but a typical branding project (strategy, identity, and basic guidelines) takes 6–10 weeks from discovery to delivery. Complex projects with extensive deliverables may take longer.

What are your pricing and payment terms?

We provide custom proposals based on project scope. Our standard approach is an initial deposit followed by milestone payments. Contact us for a tailored quote.

Do you offer photography and videography services?

Yes — we provide full photoshoot and videography services, from concept and production to post-production. We can include these as part of an integrated branding package.

Can you work with in-house marketing teams?

Absolutely. We frequently partner with in-house teams to provide strategy, creative direction, or production support — whichever you need to scale your efforts.
